401(a): Backdoor Roth
Why the first & second month format for 401(a)?
Executing a back-door roth transfer is done over the phone with Fidelity. Additionally if regular monthly deposits are made but the back-door transfer is executed less frequently, the interest earned is taxable income. Because of this inconvenience, most pilots opt to do the deposit and transfer simultaneously once or twice a year in large sums. This format allows you to determine which month you plan to make that call to Fidelity.
